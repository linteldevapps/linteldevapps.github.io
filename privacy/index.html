General Principles

Lintel Apps is committed to respecting your privacy. As a general rule across all of our apps:

  • We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or phone number
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use analytics, cookies, or tracking pixels
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties

Where an app deviates from these general principles (for example, an app that supports optional third-party integrations), those specifics will be documented in that app's own privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services — Required for app distribution on Android. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Additional third-party services used by specific apps are listed in each app's privacy policy.

Data Storage

Unless otherwise stated in an app-specific policy, data generated by our apps is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that collect or store your personal data.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.
